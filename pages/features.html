Product · Experience · Value

What the HeritageVerse app can do.

We designed HeritageVerse so that a learner can pick a monument, choose AR or VR mode, and step into a guided experience that feels like a blend of documentary, game and personal tour.

Core Value

Immersive 3D spaces + AI narration in local languages + interactive learning tasks = a history experience that is emotional, visual and easy to remember.

Sandstone Archway · Story Portal

We imagine each monument as a doorway to stories, not just a photo in a chapter.

Key features at a glance

Below are the main elements of our concept. Each feature can be expanded in the future as we move from prototype to a working product.

Mode 01

Immersive AR / VR tours

Users walk through carefully modeled 3D versions of heritage sites, look around naturally and understand scale, light and layout in a way photos cannot show.

Mode 02

AI multilingual guide

The AI guide greets the user, introduces the site and responds to simple questions. Narration is available in multiple languages to match user comfort.

Mode 03

Interactive learning layer

Hotspots, pop-up facts, timelines and short quizzes turn the virtual visit into an active learning session, useful for classrooms as well as self-paced exploration.
